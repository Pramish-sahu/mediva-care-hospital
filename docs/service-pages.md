# Service Pages — Prompt 6

Prompt 6 replaces all department placeholders with complete, reusable specialty pages.

## Completed pages

- Cardiology
- Neurology
- Orthopedics
- Pediatrics
- Dental Care
- Emergency Care

Each page includes:

- Specialty hero and breadcrumb
- Quick clinical facts
- Sticky in-page navigation
- Department overview
- Symptoms and consultation guidance
- Treatments or emergency-care pathway
- Specialist doctor cards
- Facilities and clinical support
- Accessible accordion FAQ
- EmailJS-ready appointment or follow-up form
- Medical-information disclaimer
- Full shared newsletter and footer

## Shared implementation

- `assets/css/service.css` contains the complete service-page system and specialty color variants.
- `assets/js/service-pages.js` controls accordion behavior, active in-page navigation, and future-date restrictions.
- `assets/images/departments/` contains original lightweight SVG specialty illustrations.
- New doctor portraits are stored in `assets/images/doctors/`.

## Production notes

All names, credentials, schedules, prices, services, emergency numbers, and facility claims are demonstration content. Replace them with verified hospital information before launch. Medical copy must be reviewed by the client’s clinical and legal teams for the target jurisdiction.
