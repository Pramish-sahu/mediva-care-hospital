About Dr. Mehta

Dr. Aarav Mehta is an interventional and preventive cardiologist focused on helping patients understand cardiovascular risk, make informed treatment decisions, and recover with confidence. His approach combines detailed clinical assessment with practical communication for patients and families.

Areas of expertise

His practice covers common and complex heart conditions, with particular experience in prevention and minimally invasive intervention.

Preventive cardiologyCoronary angiographyAngioplastyHypertensionHeart failureCardiac rehabilitation

Education and experience

  • DM Cardiology, All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi
  • MD Internal Medicine, Maulana Azad Medical College
  • Fellowship in Complex Coronary Intervention
  • Former consultant at a leading tertiary cardiac centre
