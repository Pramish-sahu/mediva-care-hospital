Privacy Policy

Your information deserves careful handling

This notice explains the principles that guide how information submitted through the Mediva website should be handled.

Last updated: June 28, 2026. This is portfolio demonstration content and requires jurisdiction-specific legal review before launch.

1. Overview

This privacy notice explains how Mediva Care Hospital collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use this demonstration website or contact our care teams. It is written for a static portfolio website and must be reviewed by qualified legal and clinical governance teams before production use.

2. Information we collect

We may receive contact details, appointment preferences, enquiry messages, and information you voluntarily include in forms. The public website should not be used to submit highly sensitive medical records unless a secure, approved channel is provided.

Practical note

Use official hospital channels when sharing health information, identity documents, payment information, or insurance records.

3. How information is used

Information is used to respond to enquiries, coordinate appointments, provide requested services, improve website usability, and meet legal or safety obligations. We do not sell personal information.

4. When information may be shared

Information may be shared with authorized hospital teams, service providers operating under appropriate agreements, insurers or TPAs when requested, and public authorities where legally required.

5. Security and retention

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards should protect information. Production retention periods must align with applicable healthcare, tax, insurance, and privacy requirements.

Practical note

Use official hospital channels when sharing health information, identity documents, payment information, or insurance records.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, restriction, deletion, or information about how your data is handled. Some healthcare records must be retained for legal and clinical reasons.

7. Cookies and analytics

This demo does not intentionally deploy advertising cookies. A production deployment may use essential preferences and privacy-respecting analytics after suitable consent controls are implemented.

8. Privacy contact

For privacy enquiries, email privacy@medivahospital.com or write to the Data Protection Contact, Mediva Care Hospital, Sector 18, New Delhi 110075.
