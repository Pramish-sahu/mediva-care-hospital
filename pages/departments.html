How care connects

One journey, supported at every step

Our departments share information and collaborate so patients do not have to navigate complex care alone.

1. Start with the right specialist

Our care desk helps match symptoms and concerns with the most appropriate department.

2. Complete coordinated diagnostics

Imaging, laboratory, and specialty testing are planned to reduce unnecessary visits.

3. Receive a joined-up care plan

When more than one specialty is involved, doctors review findings together.

Not sure which department you need?

Tell our care coordinators what you are experiencing, and they will guide you to the right starting point.
