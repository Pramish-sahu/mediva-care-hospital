Our story

A modern hospital designed around the full patient journey

Mediva was created to make high-quality hospital care feel clearer, calmer, and more connected.

From the first enquiry to diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up, our teams focus on communication as much as clinical precision. Patients and families receive understandable information, coordinated next steps, and access to the right expertise without unnecessary friction.

  • Multidisciplinary care planning for complex conditions
  • Modern diagnostics and evidence-led clinical protocols
  • Clear cost guidance and dedicated patient coordination
  • Comfortable spaces designed for dignity and healing
What guides us

Clinical excellence grounded in human values

Our decisions are shaped by safety, respect, accountability, and the belief that informed patients experience better care.

Our journey

Built carefully, growing responsibly

Each milestone reflects an investment in people, technology, and a better patient experience.

2014

Mediva opens its doors

A 75-bed multispecialty hospital begins with core medical, surgical, and emergency services.

2018

Advanced cardiac and neuro services

Dedicated cath lab, stroke pathway, and critical care teams strengthen time-sensitive treatment.

2022

Expanded women’s and children’s centre

Family-focused maternity, neonatal, pediatric, and women’s health services open in one integrated wing.

2026

A connected 250-bed care campus

Digital coordination, expanded diagnostics, preventive health, and rehabilitation complete the care continuum.
